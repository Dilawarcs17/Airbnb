Where you’ll be

Jibhi, Himachal Pradesh, India

★Tandi is a remote village above Jibhi, a 8 kilometer drive from the Jibhi market.

★It's a beautiful village with wheat and maize fields, pear trees, and apple orchards.

★The Himalayan subtropical pine forests are on the both sides of the road from Jibhi to Tandi.

★The treehouse is surrounded by mountainous forests on all sides.

★There's nothing nearby, other than a sparsely populated village of about 100 people.

★The waterfall originates here.

★Tandi is the most beautiful village in the Jibhi area.

★You'll have a very comfortable life here, away from everything.
